What we do

Predictions you can put a number on

Simulations and lab tests are slow and expensive. We learn from the results you already have, then predict the next one in seconds.

Every prediction comes with a range, so you can see how sure the model is. Move past what it has seen and it tells you, instead of guessing quietly.

Common questions

01Where is PI Probaligence based?+

PI Probaligence is based in Grafing bei Munich, Germany. It was founded in 2018 as a spin-off of the Institute for Modeling and High Performance Computing at Niederrhein University of Applied Sciences, and works with engineering and R&D teams internationally.

02Is PI part of CADFEM?+

Yes. The CADFEM Group acquired a stake in PI Probaligence, and PI has been part of the CADFEM Group since 2024. PI continues to develop STOCHOS and STOCHOS Flow as its own products for engineering and R&D.

03Is PI an Ansys partner?+

Yes. PI Probaligence is an official Ansys Technology Partner. STOCHOS Flow connects to Ansys workflows, reading Ansys Workbench parameters and calling the Ansys Solver node for selective validation, so the solver stays the source of truth.

04How do I request a demo?+

Use the Request a Demo button or the contact page. Bring one use case, bottleneck, or dataset, and we can outline where STOCHOS fits and what a realistic proof of concept could look like. PI often works with teams that have no in-house machine learning team.
