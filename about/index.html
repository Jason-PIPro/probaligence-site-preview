Company

Local, probabilistic AI for engineering and R&D.

PI Probaligence is an AI and machine learning company for engineering, simulation, chemistry, and R&D. It turns existing simulation, experimental, and process data into fast surrogate models with quantified uncertainty. Founded in 2018, part of the CADFEM Group, and an official Ansys Technology Partner.

Founded 2018  ·  Part of the CADFEM Group since 2024  ·  Ansys Technology Partner

What we do

Two layers, one system

STOCHOS is the probabilistic intelligence layer: prediction, optimization, and design exploration for small, noisy engineering data, built on DIM-GP. STOCHOS Flow is the visual workflow layer: a local-first, AI-assisted environment to build, automate, and export technical workflows. Together they shorten development cycles and support more reliable decisions.

From research to engineering tool

A spin-off, grown up

PI Probaligence was founded in 2018 as a spin-off of the Institute for Modeling and High Performance Computing at Niederrhein University of Applied Sciences, based in Grafing bei Munich. Its core method, DIM-GP, came out of that research and is now a mature engineering tool.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Where is PI Probaligence based?+

PI Probaligence is based in Grafing bei Munich, Germany. It was founded in 2018 as a spin-off of the Institute for Modeling and High Performance Computing at Niederrhein University of Applied Sciences, and works with engineering and R&D teams internationally.

Is PI part of CADFEM?+

Yes. The CADFEM Group acquired a stake in PI Probaligence, and PI has been part of the CADFEM Group since 2024. PI continues to develop STOCHOS and STOCHOS Flow as its own products for engineering and R&D.

Is PI an Ansys partner?+

Yes. PI Probaligence is an official Ansys Technology Partner. STOCHOS Flow connects to Ansys workflows, reading Ansys Workbench parameters and calling the Ansys Solver node for selective validation, so the solver stays the source of truth.

How do I request a demo?+

Use the Request a Demo button or the contact page. Bring one use case, bottleneck, or dataset, and we can outline where STOCHOS fits and what a realistic proof of concept could look like. PI often works with teams that have no in-house machine learning team.
