Solution / Engineering

Simulate less. Innovate more.

AI for Engineering accelerates CFD, FEM, and CAE work with probabilistic surrogate models. STOCHOS learns from solver data you already trust, predicts new variants in seconds, and quantifies confidence, so you reserve full simulations for the designs worth validating. Not a solver replacement; a fast, uncertainty-aware layer on top of it.

The bottleneck

Every new variant should not require another full solve

High-fidelity simulation stays essential. The cost is using it for every candidate, every parameter set, and every iteration. STOCHOS adds a fast probabilistic layer between your existing simulation data and the next decision, so you explore broadly with predictions and solve selectively where it counts.

Slow loops

CFD, FEM, thermal, crash, and multiphysics runs turn design exploration into a bottleneck.

Too few variants

Teams validate a fraction of the design space because each solve consumes time and hardware.

Black-box predictions

A fast prediction is not enough; engineers need to know where it is reliable and where it still needs validation.

The engineering loop

Use the solver where it adds the most value

The workflow follows the engineering loop, not a list of methods. Existing solver data trains the surrogate, STOCHOS explores in seconds, and the full solver returns only where validation or new training data is needed.

Field predictions

Predict more than a single KPI

Many engineering decisions depend on the full result field. STOCHOS predicts pressure fields, FEM stress maps, temperature distributions, and time-dependent responses, with confidence attached.

  • 2D and 3D fields. Spatially resolved outputs for new parameter combinations.
  • Signals and histories. Time series, force-displacement curves, transient behavior.
  • Confidence on the result. Quantified uncertainty decides where another solve is necessary.
Geometry-aware modeling

Model the shape, not only the parameters

Engineering data carries structure: node connectivity, spatial coordinates, changing geometry, and surface or volume fields. STOCHOS follows the physical representation rather than a flat parameter table.

STL and point clouds VTK / VTU / VTP Graph and mesh data Surface and volume fields
Design-space exploration

Explore more before committing to the next solve

Once the surrogate is trained, evaluate a broad design space quickly. Use Bayesian optimization for next-best candidates, sensitivity analysis to understand the drivers, and generative geometric AI to move beyond predefined combinations.

  • Parameter exploration. Screen operating points and design variables faster.
  • Geometry exploration. Create and compare valid candidate shapes in the learned space.
  • Trade-off decisions. Balance performance, weight, robustness, cost, and constraints.

Common questions

01How can AI accelerate CFD and FEM simulations?+

STOCHOS learns a surrogate model from existing solver data, then predicts results for new parameters or geometries in seconds. You run the full solver where it adds the most value and use fast, uncertainty-aware predictions to explore many more variants before committing to the next full solve.

02Can STOCHOS predict full fields, not just one value?+

Yes. STOCHOS handles 2D and 3D fields from CFD and FEM, as well as scalars, signals, meshes, and geometries. It can predict spatially resolved outputs for new parameter combinations, so you see more than a single KPI when comparing designs.

03How does STOCHOS work with Ansys?+

PI Probaligence is an official Ansys Technology Partner. STOCHOS Flow reads Ansys Workbench parameters and calls Ansys Solver nodes for selective validation, so fast surrogate prediction connects to the solver workflows engineering teams already use. AI fits the existing process rather than replacing it.
